Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to develop a mobile app?
Development time depends on the project complexity. Simple apps take 1-2 weeks, medium projects 2-4 weeks, and complex apps with multiple features may take 1-2 months.
What payment methods do you accept?
I accept various payment methods including bank transfer, PayPal, Western Union, and local payment methods in Iraq. Payment is typically 50% upfront and 50% on delivery.
Do you provide post-delivery support?
Yes! All packages include free support after delivery. Basic packages get 1 month, Professional get 3 months, and Enterprise get 6 months of free support for bug fixes and minor updates.
Can you work on both Android and iOS?
Yes! I use Flutter which allows me to build apps for both Android and iOS from a single codebase. This saves time and ensures consistent experience across platforms.
Do you provide the source code?
Yes, you receive full ownership of the source code upon project completion and final payment. This includes all design files, code, and documentation.
